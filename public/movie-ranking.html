The Passion of the Christ is a spectaular reproduction of the final week of Jesus Christ on earth. Directed by Mel Gibson and starring Jim Cavezil, the Passion of the Christ is the most realistic film of the passion.

Starring Mel Gibson as the brave William Wallace, hero of Scotland, this film covers the greatest feats of this almost mythic hero.

Movie 3: Jesus Revolution

A rare find in the world of cinema, Jesus Revolution covers the radical life of Chuck Smith and recounts the founding of Calvary Chapel.

Follow Dr. Jones on his brave adventures to find the long lost Ark of the Covenant before the Nazi's find it!

A medieval comedy about a failed squire turned succesful knight.
